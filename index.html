Your devices never truly switch off.

Phantom power — also called standby or vampire power — is the electricity consumed by appliances even when they appear to be off.

In India, standby power accounts for up to 10% of a household's electricity bill. A set-top box alone can draw 15 W around the clock. This tool helps you see exactly what your idle devices are costing you — in rupees, in carbon, and in trees.

Energy-Saving Tips

Small habits add up. Here are the most effective ways to cut phantom power in Indian homes.

🔌 Smart Power Strips Use smart power strips to cut power to idle entertainment systems automatically.
⏰ Wi-Fi Router Schedule your router to power down between midnight and 6 AM.
⭐ BEE Star Rating Choose BEE 5-star rated appliances — they have stricter standby power limits.
📡 Set-Top Box Your set-top box is likely your biggest standby offender — switch it off at the socket.
